PAC-MAN

Release year

more

The original Pac-Man arcade game was released in 1980 by Namco, and in the US it was distributed by Midway.

Creator

more

Pac-Man was designed by game designer Toru Iwatani, working with a small team of around nine people.

Character design

more

Pac-Man’s shape was inspired either by a pizza with a missing slice or by a very simple cartoon face with an open mouth.

Cultural icon of the 80s

more

In the 1980s Pac-Man got a cartoon series, hit song, toys, clothes, and tons of merch, becoming a symbol of video games in that era.

Arcade success

more

By the early 80s, Pac-Man became the most profitable arcade game in the US, earning billions of coins in arcade cabinets.

“Kill screen” bug

more

On the original arcade version, level 256 is glitched because of a memory overflow: half of the screen is normal, half is garbage, making the game almost unplayable.
