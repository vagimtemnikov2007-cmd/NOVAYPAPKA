Donkey Kong

The game was released in 1981, created by Nintendo and Shigeru Miyamoto.

Donkey Kong was the first game where Mario appeared, though he was originally called Jumpman.

It was the first arcade game with a real story — Mario must rescue Pauline from Donkey Kong.

Donkey Kong is considered one of the creators of the platformer genre.

Donkey Kong was originally planned as a game about Popeye the sailor, but when Nintendo couldn’t get the license, they created Mario, Pauline, and Donkey Kong instead.
